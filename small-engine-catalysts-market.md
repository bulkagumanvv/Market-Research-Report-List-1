<p><p>The small engine catalysts market is segmented into diesel-based and gasoline-based catalysts. Diesel-based catalysts primarily focus on reducing emissions from diesel engines, effectively targeting particulates and nitrogen oxides (NOx) through technologies like selective catalytic reduction (SCR). Gasoline-based catalysts, on the other hand, emphasize the reduction of carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, and nitrogen oxides, typically utilizing catalytic converters. Both types are essential for meeting stringent environmental regulations and enhancing the efficiency of small engines across various applications.</p></p>
