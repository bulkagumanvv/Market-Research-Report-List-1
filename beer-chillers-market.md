<p><ul><li>Coil Type</li><li>Box Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The beer chillers market includes two main types: coil type and box type. Coil type chillers use a coiled tube to circulate cold water or refrigerant, efficiently chilling beer as it flows through, ideal for bars or events. In contrast, box type chillers feature a compact design with the beer stored in an insulated box, where cooling elements keep the entire batch cold, suitable for home use or smaller venues. Both types enhance the beer-drinking experience by ensuring optimal temperatures.</p></p>
