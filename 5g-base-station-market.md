<p><p>The 5G base station market is primarily categorized into macro and small cell types. Macro base stations cover wide areas and are typically installed on towers or tall buildings, providing extensive network coverage and capacity. Small cells, on the other hand, are deployed in densely populated areas to enhance capacity and improve indoor connectivity. These include microcells, picocells, and femtocells, which are crucial for managing high data traffic in urban environments and ensuring seamless 5G experiences for users.</p></p>
