<p><ul><li>Liquid Transmission</li><li>Electric Transmission</li><li>Mechanical Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) devices are categorized into three main types: liquid, electric, and mechanical transmission. Liquid transmission utilizes hydraulic fluids for smooth gear transitions, ensuring optimal power delivery and efficiency. Electric transmission employs electric motors and controllers, offering precise control over torque and speed, ideal for hybrid and electric vehicles. Mechanical transmission relies on variable pulleys and belts to change gear ratios continuously, providing a seamless driving experience. Together, these technologies enhance vehicle performance and fuel efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
