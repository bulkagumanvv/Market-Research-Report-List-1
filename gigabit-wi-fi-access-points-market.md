<p><strong>The Gigabit Wi-Fi Access Points Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Band</li><li>Double Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gigabit Wi-Fi access points are categorized into single band and dual band types based on their operating frequency. Single band access points operate solely on the 2.4 GHz frequency, offering a broader coverage area but lower speeds. In contrast, dual band access points can operate on both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing higher data rates and reduced interference. This dual capability enhances performance in dense environments, making them suitable for both home and business applications, meeting diverse connectivity needs.</p></p>
