<p><p>Palletizing systems can be categorized into three main types: floor level, high level, and robotic palletizers. Floor level palletizers operate at ground height, easing the loading process and maximizing efficiency in handling lower goods. High level palletizers stack products at elevated positions, utilizing vertical space and enhancing production throughput. Robotic palletizers employ robotic arms for flexible, precise stacking, capable of handling diverse product shapes and sizes with efficiency and adaptability, making them suitable for various industries.</p></p>
