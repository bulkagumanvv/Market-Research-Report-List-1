<p><strong>The Threaded Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Thread</li><li>External Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Threaded flanges are used to connect pipes and equipment, offering versatility in various applications. The market is divided into two primary types: internal thread flanges and external thread flanges. Internal thread flanges have the threading on the inside, allowing pipes to connect directly into the flange, providing a secure seal. In contrast, external thread flanges feature external threads that enable them to screw onto corresponding pipe fittings. Both types cater to specific operational requirements and are crucial in fluid and gas handling systems.</p></p>
