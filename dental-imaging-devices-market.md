<p><p>The dental imaging devices market is segmented into 2D and 3D imaging devices. 2D imaging devices, such as traditional X-rays and digital radiography, provide flat images that help detect dental issues like cavities and bone loss. In contrast, 3D imaging devices, including cone beam computed tomography (CBCT), offer detailed volumetric images, enabling precise assessment of teeth, roots, and surrounding structures. This enhanced imagery aids in accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and improved patient outcomes, driving the growth of the dental imaging market.</p></p>
