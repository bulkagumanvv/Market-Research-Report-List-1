<p><p>The mobile casing and structural parts market features three prominent types: Non-Conductive Vacuum Metallization (NCVM), Insert Molding, and Unibody construction. </p><p>NCVM involves applying a thin metallic layer to non-conductive surfaces, enhancing aesthetic appeal while maintaining lightweight properties and preventing interference with electronic signals. Insert Molding integrates component parts into a single mold, offering design flexibility and reducing assembly time. Unibody construction creates a seamless, durable casing by shaping the entire structure from a single material piece, ensuring enhanced strength and reduced weight.</p></p>
