<p><p>The EV charge station controller market is divided into two main types: AC charging controllers and DC charging controllers. AC charging controllers manage the distribution of power from the grid to electric vehicles using alternating current, typically suited for home and slow charging stations. In contrast, DC charging controllers convert AC from the grid to direct current, allowing for faster charging speeds at public stations. Both types are essential for supporting the growing demand for electric vehicle infrastructure.</p></p>
