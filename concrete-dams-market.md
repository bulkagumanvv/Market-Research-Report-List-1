Normal Concrete Dams are traditional structures that utilize conventional concrete pouring techniques, ensuring robust performance. <p><p>The concrete dams market encompasses various applications, including gravity dams, massive head buttress dams, and arch dams. Gravity dams utilize their weight to resist water pressure, making them ideal for high water levels. Massive head buttress dams combine gravity and arch principles, efficiently supporting large volumes of water while minimizing materials used. Arch dams, designed to withstand high pressure with their curved shape, are suitable for narrow canyons. Collectively, these dam types play critical roles in hydropower generation, flood control, and water supply management.</p></p>
