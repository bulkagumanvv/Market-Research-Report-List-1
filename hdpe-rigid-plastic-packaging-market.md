<p><strong>The HDPE Rigid Plastic Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HDPE rigid plastic packaging market encompasses various manufacturing processes, each serving different needs. Extrusion involves melting plastic and shaping it into continuous profiles, ideal for containers and bottles. Injection molding forces molten plastic into molds, creating durable items with intricate designs, suitable for caps and closures. Thermoforming heats plastic sheets until pliable, then molds them into specific designs, commonly used for trays and clamshells. Each method offers unique benefits in terms of efficiency, cost, and material properties.</p></p>
