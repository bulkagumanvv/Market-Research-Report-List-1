<p><p>The non-contact encoders market encompasses three main types: magnetic, capacitive, and optical encoders. Magnetic encoders use magnetic fields to determine position and are known for their robustness and resistance to dust and moisture. Capacitive encoders rely on changes in capacitance for measurement, offering high resolution and precision in a compact design. Optical encoders utilize light signals to track position, providing excellent accuracy and speed. Each type serves diverse industrial applications, emphasizing reliability and performance.</p></p>
