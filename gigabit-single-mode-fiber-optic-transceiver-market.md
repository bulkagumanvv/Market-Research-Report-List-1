<p><p>The Gigabit Single-Mode Fiber Optic Transceiver market is primarily divided into two types: Single Mode Single Fiber Optical Transceiver and Single Mode Dual Fiber Optical Transceiver. The Single Mode Single Fiber Transceiver utilizes one fiber strand to transmit and receive signals simultaneously, enhancing efficiency and reducing installation costs. In contrast, the Single Mode Dual Fiber Transceiver employs two separate fiber strands, one for transmission and one for reception, enabling higher data rates and improved performance for long-distance communication.</p></p>
