<p><p>Enrollment Management Software (EMS) is categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. </p><p>Cloud-based EMS allows institutions to access software via the internet, facilitating scalability, remote access, and lower upfront costs. It offers flexibility and automatic updates, making it suitable for organizations seeking efficiency. </p><p>Conversely, on-premises EMS is installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. This option is often preferred by larger organizations with specific compliance requirements or existing infrastructure investments.</p></p>
