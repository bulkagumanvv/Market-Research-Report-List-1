<p><ul><li>Air-purifying Respirators</li><li>Supplied-air Respirators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The respirator market comprises various types, primarily including air-purifying respirators (APRs) and supplied-air respirators (SARs). APRs filter contaminants from the air, relying on masks or cartridges to protect users in environments with hazardous airborne substances. In contrast, SARs provide breathable air from an external source, ensuring a continuous supply in environments where air quality is compromised or toxic. Both types serve critical roles in worker safety across industries like construction, healthcare, and manufacturing.</p></p>
