<p><p>The Low Voltage DC Circuit Breaker market includes primarily two types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCB) and Molded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCB). MCBs are designed for overload and short-circuit protection in low current applications, often used in residential settings. MCCBs, on the other hand, cater to higher current applications and provide adjustable trip settings, making them suitable for industrial environments. Both types are essential for ensuring electrical safety, preventing equipment damage, and maintaining system reliability in various applications.</p></p>
