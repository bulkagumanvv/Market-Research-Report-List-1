<p><p>Flow controllers are crucial in regulating the flow of fluids and gases in various industries. The volumetric flow market focuses on measuring the volume of fluid passing through a system within a specific timeframe, making it ideal for applications requiring precise volume control. In contrast, the mass flow market measures the mass of the fluid flowing, providing accurate readings regardless of changes in temperature or pressure, and is essential in processes where mass consistency is vital, such as pharmaceuticals and food processing.</p></p>
