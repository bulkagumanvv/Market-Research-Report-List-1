<p><p>The carbohydrase market primarily includes two main types: cellulases and amylases. Cellulases are enzymes that break down cellulose, a key structural component of plant cell walls, making them vital for industries such as biofuel production and paper manufacturing. Amylases, on the other hand, catalyze the hydrolysis of starch into sugars, playing an essential role in food processing, brewing, and pharmaceuticals. Together, these enzymes are crucial in enhancing efficiency and sustainability across various applications in the biotechnology sector.</p></p>
