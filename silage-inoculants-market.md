<p><p>Silage inoculants are additives used to enhance the fermentation process and improve the quality of silage. The market is divided into two primary types: wet inoculants and dry inoculants. Wet inoculants are liquid formulations containing live bacteria, which are applied directly to the forage. They are effective in promoting rapid fermentation. In contrast, dry inoculants are powder-based, allowing for easier handling and storage. Both types aim to enhance nutrient preservation and improve animal feed efficiency.</p></p>
