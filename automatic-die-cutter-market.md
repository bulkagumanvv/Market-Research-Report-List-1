<p><p>The Automatic Die-Cutter market is primarily segmented into two types: Rotary Die Cutters and Flat Bed Die Cutters. Rotary Die Cutters utilize rotating cylindrical dies to cut and shape materials in a continuous feed process, offering high speed and efficiency, ideal for large production runs. In contrast, Flat Bed Die Cutters use a flat surface to apply pressure, allowing for intricate designs and thicker materials, making them suitable for short runs and specialty applications. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
