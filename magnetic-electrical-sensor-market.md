<p><p>The magnetic electrical sensor market comprises several types, including Hall Effect, AMR (Anisotropic Magnetoresistance), GMR (Giant Magnetoresistance), and other types. Hall Effect sensors detect magnetic fields through voltage generation, while AMR sensors respond to changes in magnetic orientation, offering high sensitivity. GMR sensors operate on the principle of significant resistance changes in magnetic fields, ideal for data storage applications. Other types may include magneto-resistive or fluxgate sensors, each serving unique applications in automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
