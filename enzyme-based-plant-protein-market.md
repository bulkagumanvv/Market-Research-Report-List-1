<p><p>The enzyme-based plant protein market includes various types such as cellulase, lactase, amylase, pectinase, and mannanases, each serving specific purposes in protein extraction and processing. Cellulase breaks down cellulose, enhancing fiber digestion, while lactase improves lactose utilization. Amylase aids in starch conversion to sugars, optimizing fermentation. Pectinase assists in plant cell wall breakdown, enhancing juice extraction from fruits. Mannanases target mannans in plant materials, increasing protein bioavailability. Other enzymes contribute to niche applications, driving innovation in plant protein processing.</p></p>
