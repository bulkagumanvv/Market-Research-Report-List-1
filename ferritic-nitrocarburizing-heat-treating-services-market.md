<p><p>Ferritic nitrocarburizing heat treating services encompass three primary market types: </p><p>1. **Salt Bath Ferritic Nitrocarburizing** involves immersing components in a molten salt bath, enhancing wear resistance and surface hardness effectively. </p><p>2. **Gaseous Ferritic Nitrocarburizing** utilizes gas mixtures at elevated temperatures to diffuse nitrogen and carbon into the steel surface, improving toughness and corrosion resistance. </p><p>3. **Plasma-Assisted Ferritic Nitrocarburizing** employs plasma to create a reactive environment, allowing for precise control over the treatment, resulting in uniform and tailored properties for advanced applications.</p></p>
