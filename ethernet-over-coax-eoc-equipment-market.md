<p><p>Ethernet over Coax (EoC) equipment consists of two main market types: Passive EoC and Active EoC. Passive EoC utilizes existing coaxial cables without electronic enhancement, providing a cost-effective solution for extending Ethernet networks, primarily in residential settings. In contrast, Active EoC employs powered devices to boost signals, enhancing performance over longer distances and enabling higher data rates. This technology is ideal for commercial applications, offering improved reliability and network management features, thereby catering to diverse connectivity needs.</p></p>
