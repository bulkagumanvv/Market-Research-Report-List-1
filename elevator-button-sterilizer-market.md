<p><p>The elevator button sterilizer market comprises two main types: internal and external sterilizers. Internal elevator button sterilizers are designed to clean and disinfect the buttons located within the elevator cabin, ensuring that high-touch surfaces are hygienic for passengers. In contrast, external elevator button sterilizers target the buttons located outside the elevator doors, providing protection for users before they enter. Both types play a crucial role in promoting cleanliness and reducing the risk of germ transmission in public spaces.</p></p>
