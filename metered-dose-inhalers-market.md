Dry powdered inhalers release powdered medication activated by inhalation, while pressurized metered dose inhalers utilize a propellant to disperse aerosolized medication. Nebulizers convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation, ideal for severe cases. Connected metered dose inhalers incorporate digital technology for tracking usage, aiding adherence and providing data for better management of respiratory conditions like asthma and COPD.</p></p>
