<p><p>The postoperative pain therapeutics market encompasses various administration routes to manage pain effectively. Intramuscular injections deliver drugs directly into the muscle, allowing for quicker absorption and relief for patients unable to take oral medications. Intravenous therapies, administered through a vein, ensure rapid delivery of analgesics, offering precise control over pain management in hospital settings. Each type caters to different patient needs and treatment scenarios.</p></p>
