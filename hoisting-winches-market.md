<p><p>Hoisting winches are classified into three main types based on their power source: manual, electric, and hydraulic. Manual winches require human effort to operate, making them ideal for smaller jobs where portability is essential. Electric winches offer automated lifting with greater speed and efficiency, suitable for medium to heavy loads. Hydraulic winches utilize hydraulic power, delivering high torque and capability for heavy-duty applications, often preferred in industrial settings. Each type serves unique operational needs across various industries.</p></p>
