<p><p>The emergency lighting batteries market includes several types based on chemistry and application. Nickel-Cadmium (NiCd) batteries are known for their longevity and robustness, making them reliable in power outages. Nickel-Metal Hydride (NiMH) batteries are more environmentally friendly and offer better energy density. Lithium-ion (Li-ion) batteries provide high energy efficiency and faster charging. Lead-acid batteries are traditional and cost-effective, commonly used in larger systems. Other battery types may include advanced technologies or specialized configurations to enhance emergency lighting performance.</p></p>
