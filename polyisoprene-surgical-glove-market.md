<p><p>The Polyisoprene Surgical Glove market is categorized into two main types: powdered sterilized surgical gloves and powder-free sterilized surgical gloves. Powdered gloves are coated with a cornstarch lubricant, aiding in easy donning but may pose allergy risks and can interfere with some surgical procedures. In contrast, powder-free gloves eliminate these concerns, offering a safer alternative while ensuring optimal grip and tactile sensitivity. Increasing demand for safety and reduced allergic reactions is driving the growth of powder-free gloves in healthcare settings.</p></p>
