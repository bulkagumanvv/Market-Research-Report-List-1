<p><p>The biological fermentor market is primarily categorized into solid and liquid fermentors, based on the medium used for microbial growth. Solid fermentors are designed for processes involving solid substrates, typically used in the production of enzymes, organic acids, and fermented foods, allowing for higher product concentrations and lower water usage. In contrast, liquid fermentors utilize liquid media, enabling easier control of environmental conditions and mass transfer, making them ideal for large-scale production of pharmaceuticals, biofuels, and recombinant proteins.</p></p>
