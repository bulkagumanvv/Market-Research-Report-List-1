<p><p>The external beam radiotherapy market includes several advanced techniques. Intensity-modulated radiotherapy (IMRT) delivers varying radiation doses, targeting tumors while sparing healthy tissue. Image-guided radiotherapy (IGRT) enhances precision by using imaging technologies for accurate tumor localization. TomoTherapy combines CT imaging with IMRT for effective treatment delivery. Stereotactic therapy delivers high doses with precision for small tumors. 3D conformal radiotherapy shapes radiation beams to match tumor outlines. Particle beam therapy uses protons or heavy ions for targeted treatment, while volumetric modulated arc therapy offers continuous, precise delivery through rotational movement.</p></p>
