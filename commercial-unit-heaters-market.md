<p><p>The commercial unit heaters market is segmented into four main types: gas-fired, hydronic, electric, and others. Gas-fired heaters utilize natural gas for efficient heating, making them popular for large spaces. Hydronic heaters circulate hot water through pipes, providing consistent warmth and energy efficiency. Electric heaters rely on electricity to generate heat, ideal for areas lacking gas lines. The "others" category encompasses alternative heating solutions, such as infrared and portable units, catering to diverse commercial heating needs.</p></p>
