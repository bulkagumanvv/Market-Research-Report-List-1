<p><ul><li>Multi-Port Injector</li><li>Single-Port Injector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air injectors market comprises two main types: multi-port injectors and single-port injectors. Multi-port injectors deliver air into the intake manifold at multiple points, enhancing combustion efficiency and reducing emissions. They are commonly used in high-performance and modern engines. In contrast, single-port injectors introduce air at a single point, suitable for simpler engine designs and older models where cost-effectiveness is prioritized. Both types play crucial roles in improving fuel efficiency and reducing environmental impact in automotive applications.</p></p>
