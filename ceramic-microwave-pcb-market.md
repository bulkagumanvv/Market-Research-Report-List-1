<p><p>The Ceramic Microwave PCB market can be categorized into two main types: Double Layer Boards and Multi-layer Boards. Double Layer Boards consist of two conductive layers, making them suitable for simple circuits with moderate complexity and improved thermal performance. In contrast, Multi-layer Boards feature three or more layers, allowing for more complex designs and higher density interconnections. This structure enhances performance in high-frequency applications, making Multi-layer Boards ideal for sophisticated devices requiring superior signal integrity and reduced electromagnetic interference.</p></p>
