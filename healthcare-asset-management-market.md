<p><p>Healthcare asset management involves various technologies for tracking and managing medical equipment and resources. RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) utilizes electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects, enhancing inventory accuracy. RTLS (Real-Time Location Systems) provides precise location data for assets in real-time, improving asset utilization. Ultrasound technology uses sound waves for tracking, offering high accuracy in close environments. Infrared systems employ light beams to monitor asset movement and locations, ensuring efficient resource management and minimizing losses in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
