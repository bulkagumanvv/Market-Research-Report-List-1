<p><strong>The Sonar Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam Scanning Sonar System</li><li>Multi-beam Sonar System</li><li>Side Scan Sonar System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sonar systems are categorized into several types based on their scanning capabilities. Single beam scanning sonar systems use a single acoustic beam to measure depths, ideal for simple applications. Multi-beam sonar systems emit multiple beams simultaneously, allowing for detailed seafloor mapping and better coverage. Side scan sonar systems produce images of the seabed by emitting sonar to the sides, useful for detecting underwater objects. Other market types include advanced sonar technologies like Synthetic Aperture Sonar and underwater communication systems, enhancing exploration and research.</p></p>
