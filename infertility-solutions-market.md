<p><p>The infertility solutions market comprises various types aimed at helping individuals conceive. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves injecting a single sperm directly into an egg, enhancing fertilization chances. Surrogacy allows another woman to carry a pregnancy for intended parents. Artificial Insemination involves directly placing sperm in the reproductive tract. Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) places sperm into the uterus during ovulation, while Intracervical Insemination places sperm at the cervix. Other methods include ovarian stimulation and fertility medications. Each approach caters to diverse reproductive needs.</p></p>
